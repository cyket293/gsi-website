Non-intrusive monitoring of storage tanks, pipelines, and flare stacks.

The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Mitigate catastrophic risk and maintain stringent operational compliance through objective, high-fidelity thermal data collection.

01. Asset Targeting & Compliance

We coordinate with plant managers to identify critical infrastructure, adhering to all site-specific safety and explosive-environment protocols.

02. Radiometric Scanning

Using specialized thermal sensors, we capture the temperature differentials of tank fluid levels, refractory linings, and high-pressure pipelines.

03. Anomaly Detection

Our experts analyze the thermograms to detect hidden leaks, refractory breakdown, sludge buildup, and abnormal thermal signatures.

04. Actionable Intelligence

We deliver a comprehensive diagnostic report, providing your maintenance and engineering teams with the precise data needed to execute targeted interventions.
