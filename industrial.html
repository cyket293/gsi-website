The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Improve operational efficiency by integrating high-fidelity thermal data into your internal preventative maintenance workflows.

01. Site Inspection & Evaluation

We assess your manufacturing environment to target mechanical systems, HVAC, and heavily utilized infrastructure.

02. Infrared Data Collection

Integrating both aerial and handheld sensors, we record thermal signatures of machinery, pipes, and structures.

03. Analysis & Identification

We review the thermal imagery to identify abnormal mechanical friction, heat loss, and energy inefficiencies.

04. Objective Reporting

We provide a data-rich diagnostic report of thermal findings to support and streamline your internal preventative maintenance protocols.
