Privacy Policy

Data protection standards for Gulf South Infrared, LLC.

1. Identity

This Privacy Policy applies to Gulf South Infrared, LLC, a limited liability company registered in the State of Louisiana, doing business as GSI Thermal and GSI ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information via our digital platforms.

2. Operational Data Security

GSI Thermal operates in high-security industrial environments. We adhere to strict non-disclosure regarding client asset data. Thermal imagery, radiometric data files, and site-specific GPS coordinates collected during inspections are treated as proprietary client data. We do not share, sell, or publicly display site-specific inspection data without express written consent from the asset owner.

3. Information Collection

We collect information submitted voluntarily through our "Operations Desk" and "Careers" forms, including names, corporate affiliations, email addresses, and phone numbers. This data is used solely for the purpose of operational scheduling, recruitment, and client communication.

4. Analytics & Cookies

Our website may use standard cookies to monitor site traffic and performance. This data is anonymized and used to improve the technical performance of our digital infrastructure.

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact our administrative office at:
