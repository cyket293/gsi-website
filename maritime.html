Forensic thermal analysis of vessels and offshore rig infrastructure.

The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Prevent catastrophic equipment failure and marine fires by identifying mechanical friction and electrical resistance in extreme environments.

01. Vessel & Rig Assessment

We consult with chief engineers to map out critical propulsion systems, switchboards, and heavy deck machinery for targeted inspection.

02. Marine-Grade Data Collection

Operating within tight, complex compartments, we capture high-fidelity thermal signatures of live electrical panels and rotating equipment.

03. Critical Fault Identification

We analyze the temperature gradients to uncover bearing degradation, exhaust leaks, phase imbalances, and overloaded circuits.

04. Diagnostic Deliverables

We produce an objective defect report, giving your marine engineers the precise locations required to perform preventative maintenance while at sea or in port.
