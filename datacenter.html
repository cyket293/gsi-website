Data Center Infrastructure Diagnostics

Precision thermal monitoring of server racks, UPS systems, and CRAC units.

The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Prevent catastrophic IT hardware failure and optimize cooling efficiency through high-resolution thermal mapping.

White Space & Power Evaluation

We coordinate with facility operators to target high-density server aisles, power distribution units (PDUs), and backup battery arrays.

Non-Invasive Thermal Capture

Using handheld thermography, we safely record the heat signatures of live server racks, electrical connections, and under-floor airflow.

Hot-Spot & Airflow Analysis

We evaluate the data to detect localized overheating, UPS battery resistance, loose electrical terminations, and inefficient cold-aisle containment.

Objective Reporting

We deliver a precise diagnostic assessment, empowering your IT and facility teams to rebalance loads, repair connections, and ensure zero downtime.
