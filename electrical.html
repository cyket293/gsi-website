Electrical Systems Diagnostics

Non-destructive infrared scanning of panels, switchgear, and motors.

The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Detect overheating components before they lead to critical failures, unexpected downtime, or electrical fires.

01. Site Inspection & Evaluation

We review your facility to identify critical switchgear, panels, and heavy electrical components for diagnostic scanning.

02. Infrared Data Collection

Using advanced handheld thermal cameras, we safely capture temperature differentials across live electrical systems.

03. Analysis & Identification

Our experts analyze the thermal data, detecting dangerous hotspots, overloads, and loose connections.

04. Objective Reporting

We deliver a comprehensive diagnostic report detailing exact abnormality locations for your licensed electrical team to address.
