Predictive Electrical Diagnostics

Identify resistance and thermal anomalies in live electrical systems.

The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Detect overheating components, phase imbalances, and loose terminations before they lead to critical failures, arcing, or unexpected downtime.

Asset Evaluation

We coordinate with your facility managers to identify high-load connection points, switchgear, and critical distribution panels for inspection.

Non-Contact Infrared Scanning

Operating safely outside the arc flash boundary, we capture the temperature differentials and thermal signatures of live electrical systems under normal load.

Anomaly Identification

We analyze the data to detect early indicators of failure, including high-resistance terminations, overloaded circuits, and degrading contactors.

Diagnostic Reporting

We provide a detailed thermal report documenting the exact location and severity of each hotspot, enabling your licensed electricians to prioritize preventative maintenance.
