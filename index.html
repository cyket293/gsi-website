01. Site Inspection & Evaluation

02. Infrared Data Collection

03. Analysis & Identification

Solar Infrastructure

Comprehensive string and cell-level analysis to maximize the output, safety, and longevity of industrial solar arrays.

01. Site Inspection & Evaluation

We map out the industrial solar array to plan an efficient and complete aerial thermal scan based on irradiance levels.

02. Infrared Data Collection

Utilizing radiometric aerial thermography, we capture high-fidelity temperature data across all panels and strings.

03. Analysis & Identification

04. Objective Reporting

We provide a precise diagnostic report of underperforming assets, allowing your technicians to restore optimal energy output.
