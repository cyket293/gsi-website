Operational Safety

Rigorous flight planning and industrial hazard mitigation.

Safety Management System (SMS)

We operate in high-consequence environments where safety is the gatekeeper to execution. Our protocols are designed to integrate seamlessly with client HSE requirements.

Zero-Compromise Approach

Gulf South Infrared adheres to a strict "Safety First" doctrine. Before any rotor spins, we conduct a comprehensive job site analysis to identify electromagnetic interference, aerial obstructions, and ground-based hazards. We maintain full compliance with FAA Part 107 regulations, ensuring all operations remain within legal and safe flight envelopes.

Our pilots are trained to respect the specific PPE and communication protocols of active industrial sites, from petrochemical refineries to active construction zones. We do not just fly; we integrate into your operational workflow.

FAA Compliance

All flights are conducted by FAA Part 107 Certified Commercial Drone Pilots, strictly adhering to airspace authorizations and altitude restrictions.

Risk Assessment

Mandatory pre-flight risk assessments (JSA/JHA) are performed on-site to account for weather, radio interference, and personnel movement.

Site Integration

We adhere to all client-mandated safety stand-downs, PPE requirements (FRC, H2S monitors), and intrinsically safe zone restrictions.
