The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Optimize facility energy consumption and reduce operational overhead by identifying unseen thermal bridges and envelope failures.

Environmental Baseline

We assess the commercial structure to ensure optimal indoor/outdoor temperature differentials (Delta-T) for maximum scanning accuracy.

Comprehensive Thermal Imaging

Utilizing aerial and handheld thermography, we map the exterior facades, roofing interfaces, and interior perimeters.

Forensic Analysis

We review the infrared data to pinpoint missing insulation, moisture intrusion, and significant HVAC exfiltration pathways.

Objective Defect Mapping

We provide a data-rich report detailing exact areas of energy loss, empowering your contractors to seal the envelope and improve HVAC efficiency.
