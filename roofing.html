Commercial Roofing Diagnostics

Non-destructive moisture detection and insulation analysis.

The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Identify hidden moisture entrapment through thermal contrast to prevent costly structural degradation and streamline targeted repairs.

01. Site Inspection & Protocol Design

We evaluate your roof envelope to determine the optimal environmental conditions for maximizing thermal contrast during the scanning process.

02. Radiometric Data Collection

Utilizing enterprise aerial thermography, we capture high-resolution thermal imagery across the entire membrane, detecting the specific heat signatures of trapped water.

03. Forensic Thermal Analysis

Our experts analyze the thermograms to differentiate between harmless surface anomalies and true subsurface moisture entrapment or insulation degradation.

04. Objective Defect Mapping

We deliver a comprehensive, data-rich report that pinpoints the exact locations of compromised roofing, allowing your contractors to execute precise, cost-effective repairs.
