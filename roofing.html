The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Select a phase below to explore our forensic moisture detection process.

Site Inspection & Protocol Design

Before deployment, we evaluate your roof envelope to determine the optimal environmental conditions for scanning. This involves analyzing the roof membrane type (TPO, EPDM, Built-Up), insulation composition, and current weather patterns to ensure maximum "Delta-T" (thermal contrast) between dry and wet substrate.

Radiometric Data Collection

Utilizing enterprise aerial thermography, we execute a pre-programmed autonomous flight path to capture high-resolution thermal imagery across 100% of the membrane. This ensures consistent angle and distance, detecting the specific heat capacitance signatures of trapped subsurface water.

Forensic Thermal Analysis

Data is analyzed by certified thermographers to differentiate between false positives (like surface reflections, HVAC exhaust, or standing water) and true subsurface moisture entrapment. We isolate the thermal anomalies that indicate insulation degradation and structural risk.

Objective Defect Mapping

We deliver a comprehensive forensic report that pinpoints the exact GPS coordinates of every compromised section. This allows your roofing contractor to surgical remove and replace only the wet insulation, rather than quoting you for a complete roof replacement.
