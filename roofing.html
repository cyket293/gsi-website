The GSI Diagnostic Protocol

Identify subsurface moisture entrapment and thermal anomalies to prevent costly structural failures and streamline insurance claims.

01. Site Inspection & Evaluation

Our team assesses your roof envelope to identify critical areas and optimal conditions for aerial thermal imaging scans.

02. Infrared Data Collection

Using high-resolution enterprise drones, we capture minute temperature variations across the entire roofing system.

03. Analysis & Identification

We perform forensic analysis on the thermal images, detecting subsurface moisture entrapment and insulation breakdown.

04. Objective Reporting

We provide a detailed diagnostic report mapping all thermal abnormalities, empowering your roofing contractors to execute targeted repairs.
